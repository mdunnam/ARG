Somnatek Sleep Health Center

Somnatek Sleep Health Center provided diagnostic sleep testing and comprehensive follow-up care for patients throughout Harrow County from 2006 to 2014. Our clinical team offered in-lab polysomnography, home sleep testing, CPAP titration and compliance services, and access to longitudinal sleep health research programs.

This site remains available for former patients seeking archived educational materials, billing contact information, and records transfer notices. New appointment requests cannot be accommodated.

Services Provided

  • Diagnostic polysomnography (in-lab)
  • Home sleep apnea testing
  • CPAP and BiPAP titration
  • Compliance monitoring and follow-up
  • Insomnia evaluation and behavioral consultation
  • Narcolepsy and hypersomnia assessment
  • Research study participation (enrollment closed)

Patient Information

Former patients with questions about archived records, insurance billing, or referral documentation should consult the closure notice page for contact and transfer information.

Educational materials related to sleep hygiene, CPAP use, and common sleep disorders remain available on the Patient Resources page for reference.

Records Transfer: Patient records from Somnatek Sleep Health Center were transferred to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC in accordance with applicable state and federal regulations. Former patients may submit records requests through Dorsal Health Holdings. Somnatek Sleep Health Center no longer maintains or distributes patient records directly.

Research Participation

Somnatek participated in collaborative sleep research with Wexler University from 2008 through 2013. Study enrollment is closed and no new participants are being accepted. Former study participants with questions about their participation records should consult the Research page.
