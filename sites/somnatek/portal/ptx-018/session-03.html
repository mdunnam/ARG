Patient Recall Narrative

Patient reported a slight increase in recall clarity compared to previous sessions. When asked to describe any spatial or environmental elements, the patient said they remembered 'a long hallway.' Could not describe the hallway further — no details about lighting, color, width, or contents. Patient appeared uncertain whether this was a memory of a specific place or simply a generalized spatial impression.

Technician Notes

Slight increase from baseline. First spatial element reported: 'a long hallway.' Patient could not elaborate. Did not appear distressed. No further prompting per protocol. Rating increased to 3/5.

Physician Review

Environmental element introduced. Monitor for consistency in subsequent sessions. Do not prompt for additional spatial detail.
