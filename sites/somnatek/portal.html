Patient Portal

Portal Archived This portal has been archived as of September 18, 2014. Active patient records management has transferred to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC. Former patients may use their assigned participant ID to access archived study materials retained under the terms of the records transfer agreement.

If you participated in the Wexler University longitudinal sleep recall study conducted at this facility between 2008 and 2013, you may use your assigned participant ID to access your archived recall summaries below.

Participant IDs were provided at study enrollment and appear on your original intake documentation in the format PTX-###. If you do not have your participant ID, contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC.

This portal does not provide access to standard clinical records, test results, or billing history. For clinical records requests, contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC. This system is provided for research study archive access only and is not monitored by clinical staff.
