Notice of Clinic Closure

Issued: July 14, 2014  |  Effective: September 18, 2014

Dear Former Patient,

Somnatek Sleep Health Center ceased all clinical operations on September 18, 2014. We wish to inform you of the arrangements that have been made regarding your patient records and any outstanding account matters.

Records Transfer

Patient records from Somnatek Sleep Health Center have been transferred to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC in accordance with applicable state and federal requirements. Dorsal Health Holdings LLC will maintain your records for the legally required retention period.

To request copies of your records, please contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC directly:

Dorsal Health Holdings LLC
Records Request Department
P.O. Box 1140, Harrow County

Phone: (740) 555-0241
Email: records@dorsalhealth.com

Please include your full name, date of birth, and approximate dates of service when submitting a records request. Allow 15 to 20 business days for processing.

Billing and Insurance

All outstanding billing matters, explanation of benefits questions, and insurance coordination inquiries should be directed to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC at the contact information above. Somnatek Sleep Health Center no longer processes billing inquiries directly.

If you received a billing statement dated after September 18, 2014 bearing a Somnatek return address, please contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC to confirm the status of your account.

Prescription and Referral Continuity

If you require continuity of CPAP or other durable medical equipment, or if you need a referral to a current sleep medicine provider, please contact your primary care physician. Somnatek Sleep Health Center cannot provide new prescriptions, referrals, or clinical consultations.

Research Study Participants

Former participants in the Wexler University longitudinal sleep recall study should direct any questions regarding their participation records to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC. Research participation documentation is maintained separately from standard clinical records and is subject to applicable research retention guidelines.

Please note: Research participation records are classified as active archive under the terms of the records transfer agreement. Requests for research participation documentation may require additional processing time.

Contact

The Somnatek telephone number (404) 551-4145 and fax number (404) 551-4146 are no longer monitored. The email address records@somnatek.org is checked periodically for records-related inquiries and will remain available through December 31, 2015.

We thank you for your trust in Somnatek Sleep Health Center during our years of operation.

Sincerely,

Somnatek Sleep Health Center Administrative Office

This notice was mailed to all patients with active records as of July 14, 2014. It is provided here for the convenience of former patients who may not have received the original mailing. This page does not constitute legal notice for purposes of the records transfer.
