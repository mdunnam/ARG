Insurance & Billing

Important: Somnatek Sleep Health Center ceased clinical and administrative operations on September 18, 2014. Billing inquiries, outstanding balance disputes, and records requests related to dates of service before closure should be directed to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC, which assumed responsibility for Somnatek administrative records as of November 3, 2014.

The following information is provided for reference by former patients who may need to reconstruct billing records or respond to unresolved insurance correspondence from the period during which Somnatek was operational.

Research study participants enrolled under Protocol 7A were billed separately under a clinical research billing track. Research participant billing was handled through Wexler University. Former research participants with billing questions should contact Wexler University’s Office of Research Administration.

Prior Authorization

Somnatek required prior authorization from most major carriers before scheduling in-lab polysomnography or MSLT procedures. The authorization process typically required a referring physician letter documenting clinical indication, a completed sleep questionnaire (ESS and STOP-BANG or equivalent), and a signed patient consent.

Authorization numbers were documented in the patient record and on the Explanation of Benefits (EOB) returned by the insurer. If you received a denial and need to reconstruct the authorization record, contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC for a copy of your clinical file.

The prior authorization request form used by Somnatek is available on the Patient Resources page.

Outstanding Balances

Somnatek Sleep Health Center transferred all open accounts receivable to Dorsal Health Holdings LLC as of November 3, 2014. Patients who received a billing statement dated after this date should contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC directly. Somnatek is no longer able to process payments, issue adjustments, or respond to billing disputes.

If you believe you received a statement in error or have documentation of a payment that was not reflected in your account, contact Dorsal Health Holdings LLC in writing at the address on your statement.
